Our programs provide a clear pathway that starts with basic character qualities and builds up to real-world responsibility, leadership and job opportunities. In the context of a safe relationship, working towards goals like entrepreneurship is not only motivating for the kids we help, but has the potential to change their lives, the lives of their families, and their communities as a whole.

Providing life-changing experiences for at-risk youth

Our summer programs are provided as a free resource for over 40 partner organizations in the Greenville area. We provide these adventures as a way to challenge students outside of their comfort zones and provide them with experiences they often never get to try.

Developing them as leaders

Our Adventure Team programs are year-round discipleship programs designed to teach at-risk middle and high school youth to be leaders in their communties. Through Bible-based teaching, we train them to respect each other, develop themselves physically and plan for their future of college or the workforce.

Hiring them to lead their peers

As our students grow through the Adventure Team program, they progress through a series of levels that each give them more responsibilities as they achieve goals tailored to each student. The ultimate goal being to hire them as Jr Guides or help them start their own youth-run businesses

Summer Adventure Programs

Our summer adventure programs provide free outdoor activies for thousands of kids each each year - mountain biking, whitewater rafting, rock climbing, hiking and backpacking - as well as indoor rock climbing. These activities provide a backdrop for gospel teaching that allows kids to escape the chaotic life circumstances they are accustomed to dealing with.

We partner with over 40 organizations around the state of South Carolina - anything from group homes or after-school care centers to residential drug rehab facilities or department of juvenile justice programs. Our staff specializes in creating opportunities that suit each group's individual and specialized needs.

This program fills a gap in most organization's summer programs and allow them to serve their communities more effectively.

Adventure Teams

Through serving thousands of at-risk students each year during the summer, we meet a number of students who connect with our staff or outdoor activities and want to learn more. Our organization has found the main motivators for these students to be relationships and responsibility.

Adventure Teams are mentoring and discipleship groups that provide a framework for students to excel in school, at home, in their community and at GOAT. Adventure Teams meet together for two hours each week and students get individual attention and goal setting sessions throughout the week.

Adventure Teams also provide a clear level system for students to measure their growth in success with the ultimate goal of earning more responsibility around GOAT and eventually a job as a Jr Guide.
