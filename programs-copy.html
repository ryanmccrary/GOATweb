We provide a clear pathway that starts with basic character qualities and builds up to real-world responsibility, leadership and job opportunities. In the context of a safe relationship, working towards goals like entrepreneurship is not only motivating for the kids we help, but has the potential to change their lives, the lives of their families, and their communities as a whole.

Providing life-changing experiences for at-risk youth

Our summer programs are provided as a free resource for over 40 partner organizations in the Greenville area. We provide these adventures as a way to challenge students outside of their comfort zones and provide them with experiences they often never get to try.

Developing them as leaders

Our Adventure Team programs are year-round discipleship programs designed to teach at-risk middle and high school youth to be leaders in their communties. Through Bible-based teaching, we train them to respect each other, develop themselves physically and plan for their future of college or the workforce.

Hiring them to lead their peers

As our students grow through the Adventure Team program, they progress through a series of levels that each give them more responsibilities as they achieve goals tailored to each student. The ultimate goal being to hire them as Jr Guides or help them start their own youth-run businesses
